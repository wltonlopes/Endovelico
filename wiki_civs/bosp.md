# Bosporan Kingdom

## História

This was an ancient Greco-Scythian state located on the Crimean Peninsula and the Taman Peninsula, centered around the Cimmerian Bosphorus (present-day Strait of Kerch). It was one of the first Hellenistic states, adopting Greek language and civilization under aristocratic leadership.
