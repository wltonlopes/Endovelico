# Paiutes

## História
Numic-speaking peoples of the Great Basin (Nevada/Utah/California). Semi-nomadic foragers practicing seasonal pine-nut harvesting and irrigation-aided farming. Lived in wikiups; crafted intricate baskets.
     Traded with Ancestral Puebloans.
