# Khoikhoi

## História

The Khoikhoi are indigenous peoples of Southern Africa, known for their unique click       languages and distinct cultures. Comprising the Khoikhoi (herders) and San (hunter-gatherers),       they have inhabited the region for thousands of years. Their rich traditions include rock art       and deep spiritual connections to the land, reflecting a profound understanding of their       environment.
