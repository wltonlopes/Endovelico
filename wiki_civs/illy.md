# Illyrians

## História
Indo-European tribes in Balkans (Albania/Montenegro). Skilled shipbuilders and pirates harassing Greek/Roman ships. Organized in kingdoms (e.g., Ardiaei); Queen Teuta fought Rome (229 BC)
