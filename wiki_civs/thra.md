# Thracians

## História
The Thracians were an ancient Indo-European people who inhabited the Balkan region, encompassing modern-day Bulgaria, Romania, Greece, and parts of Turkey. They were known for their fierce warrior culture and their reputation as skilled mercenaries. While often labeled "barbarians" by the Greeks and Romans, their culture included sophisticated poetry and music.
