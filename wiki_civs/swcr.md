# Creek

## História

The Creek, or Muscogee, civilization was a confederation of Native American tribes, primarily Muskogean-speaking groups, in the southeastern United States. They formed a loose coalition of towns that existed in Georgia, Alabama, and northern Florida. The Creek civilization, historically known as the Creek Confederacy, was a dynamic entity that evolved over time, incorporating various groups and undergoing transformations, including the adoption of Euro-American practices like slavery.
